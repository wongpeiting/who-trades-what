Data dictionary

Every column in disclosure_line_items, with definitions:

Column Type Definition
item_idintegerUnique row identifier.
official_idintegerForeign key to the officials table.
official_nametextOfficial's full name, normalised (Last, First).
titletextOfficial title as it appears on the filing cover page.
partytextPolitical party affiliation (Republican / Democrat / Independent).
agencytextDepartment or agency the official leads.
source_filetextFilename of the PDF filing this row was extracted from.
form_typetextOGE form type: 278-T (periodic transaction report) or 278e (annual/nominee).
parttextSection of the form (e.g. Part 5 = holdings, Part 7 = transactions, Part 2 = income).
item_kindtexttransaction or holding.
ownertextSelf / Spouse / Joint — from 278e Part 5. Null on 278-T transactions (the form carries no owner column).
raw_descriptiontextSecurity or asset description exactly as it appears on the form (may include tickers, company names, asset class notes).
tickertextExchange ticker — from the filer's parenthetical (e.g. "(EWJ)") or a distinctive-name match to SEC EDGAR. Null for municipal/corporate bonds and private funds, which are left unresolved rather than force-matched.
sectortextGICS sector of the security, where resolved.
txn_typetextTransaction type: Purchase, Sale, Exchange, or null for holdings.
txn_datedateTransaction date (YYYY-MM-DD). Null for holdings.
value_minintegerLower bound of the reported value bracket, in USD.
value_maxintegerUpper bound of the reported value bracket, in USD.
value_brackettextHuman-readable value range as printed on the form (e.g. "$15,001 – $50,000").
income_typetextIncome category (e.g. dividends, capital gains) — populated only for income rows.
income_amounttextReported income amount or range — populated only for income rows.
eifboolean1 if the asset is held in an Excepted Investment Fund (underlying holdings not reportable).
is_preconfirmationboolean1 if the holding comes from a nominee (pre-confirmation) 278e; portfolio may have been divested.
disclosure_lag_daysintegerDays between transaction date and filing date. Statutory limit is 30–45 days. Null for annual-form transactions where the lag is unverifiable.
before_tariff_pauseboolean1 if the transaction date is 2025-04-08 — the day before Trump paused tariffs — and was disclosed late.
agency_pays_companyboolean1 if the official's agency has any USASpending contract with the company being traded.
agency_core_contractorboolean1 if the agency's recorded contract awards to the company are ≥$100 million — a "material" contractor relationship.
regulates_own_sectorboolean1 if the security's GICS sector falls within the agency's regulatory remit (a documented judgment call; see Methodology).
near_agency_actionboolean1 if the transaction date falls within seven days of a dated action by the official's own agency — a Federal Register rule or notice, or a tariff action. A reporting lead, not proof the action bears on the company traded.
source_urltextLink to the source filing PDF — a public OGE URL where one exists, otherwise the filing served locally at /source.

Officials table

The officials table (36 rows) links official_id to full name, title, party, agency, appointment type, prior office, and external identifiers (bioguide_id, wikipedia). It is not included in the bulk download above but is available via the official profile pages.
