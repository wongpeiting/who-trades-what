Who disclosed the most transactions and the biggest holdings, and who files their disclosures late?

{# fairness framing #}
These numbers measure disclosure, not trading behaviour. Coverage is uneven. Most Cabinet officials have not yet filed their annual reports, which are due around mid-2026, so their totals come almost entirely from periodic 278-T reports. Only three officials have filed a 2026 annual, and President Trump's is by far the largest. The transaction-count board below therefore ranks everyone on the periodic reports alone, the one basis they share, with Trump's annual shown separately. A low number usually means an official has not yet fully disclosed, not that they do not trade.
