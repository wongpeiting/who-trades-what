The money trail  🪙  through Trump's cabinet

Explore the personal finances of the President and his lieutenants, as disclosed in the filings they must make by law.

What am I looking at?

Self-enrichment has been a persistent question of Trump's presidency, and the law gives the public a way to check it. The Ethics in Government Act of 1978 requires the President and senior executive-branch officials to disclose what they own, earn and trade. This database collects those filings for {{ officials_list|length }} of them — the President and 35 of his senior appointees — in one searchable place.

They file two forms:

  • OGE 278e — an annual report of everything held and earned.
  • OGE 278-T — a report of each stock trade over $1,000, due within 45 days (required since the 2012 STOCK Act).

Every row is one of two kinds:

  • Transaction — a purchase or sale of a security, with the trade date.
  • Holding — an asset the filer owns, with its value range and any income it earned.

To each line we added the security's ticker, the federal contracts the official's own agency awards and flags for conflicts and late disclosures (agency contractor the official traded a company their own agency pays $100 million or more in federal contracts; late filed more than 45 days after the trade, past the statutory limit; pre–tariff-pause one of Trump's 327 trades on April 8, 2025, the day before he paused his tariffs).

The data covers transactions dated on or after Jan. 20, 2025 (the start of the term), captured in a snapshot on July 5, 2026.

Hot topics

Traded just before the tariff pause
The President's accounts bought 327 stocks on April 8, 2025 — the day before he paused his own tariffs — and reported them 447 days after the trade.
Traded in an industry they regulate
Energy Secretary Chris Wright, who founded Liberty Energy, and Interior Secretary Doug Burgum traded oil, gas and pipeline stocks in sectors their own departments oversee.
Did the timing pay off?
Across more than 10,000 priced trades, 46.9% beat the S&P 500 over the next 90 days, with a handful of large outliers.
Traded next to an official action
Over 3,000 trades fall within a week of a dated action by the trader's own agency.
Trump's crypto income
World Liberty Financial token sales and wallet income total roughly $592 million in exact figures on the President's annual filing, alongside his $TRUMP meme-coin royalties.
